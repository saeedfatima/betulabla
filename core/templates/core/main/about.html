About Betul Abla Foundation

Committed to Empowering Communities and Transforming Lives

Who We Are

At Betul Abla Foundation, we are dedicated to creating lasting change in the lives of underprivileged communities. Our organization supports orphans, develops borehole projects for access to clean water, and runs feeding initiatives to ensure that every community member has a chance to thrive.

We are inspired by proven humanitarian models and are committed to transparency, accountability, and effective interventions that empower individuals and transform communities.

Our Mission

To empower communities by providing essential resources, support, and opportunities for sustainable development. We focus on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment initiatives that pave the way for a brighter future.

Our History

Founded with a vision to transform lives, Betul Abla Foundation began as a small community initiative and has grown into a reputable organization impacting thousands of lives across Kano. Our journey is marked by dedication, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of a better tomorrow.

Over the years, our programs have expanded to include water sanitation projects, educational support, and community feeding projects that cater to the unique needs of each community.

Our Impact

Through our dedicated initiatives, we have:

  • Supported over 500 orphans with comprehensive care and education.
  • Developed numerous borehole projects ensuring clean water access in 44 local governments.
  • Conducted impactful feeding projects during Ramadan and Eid, benefitting hundreds of families.
